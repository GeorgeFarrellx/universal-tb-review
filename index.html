Amber-striped rows are the 74 near-duplicate groups that need your call. On each one, click Collapse or Keep (the stripe turns green once decided). Pressing Collapse shows the heading the group will become — the default is the spelling shared by the most entities, and where your working simplified list (or a prefix-strip) offers a shorter name, a dashed green chip suggests it: click the chip to use it. To use any other name, just type it in the box (e.g. type Sales and the group collapses into “Sales”); the preview updates as you type. Rows with no decision to make can be renamed too — every white row has a rename box (and a chip where a simpler name is available), so you can turn “Cost of sales - Purchases” into just “Purchases” — or tick Confirmed as is to keep the original name and include the row in your “changes only” paste. The Still to review filter shows whatever you haven't yet decided, renamed or ticked, so you can work down the list in order. The box also takes plain notes or a typed decision instead of the buttons. Your choices are saved in this browser as you go — when you're done, use Copy decisions for Claude and paste the result back into the chat.
